Course Design in Figma

Course for future designers. In this direction, we study the basics of design and draw. We create various design projects. On design, we give the guys a base for further development and becoming ui designers.

What do we teach?

First of all, we study the Figma program. This is a modern and relevant software that is used by designers and companies to develop interfaces and create vector graphics. Figma also has the ability to create banners, logos, stickers and work with fonts. .

Your questions

What do UI designers do? - UI designers create interface designs for programs, websites and applications.

What about artistic skills? - Of course, on the design course we draw a lot and get acquainted with the base of any profession related to drawing and design. For example - colors, palettes, perspective, contrast, symmetry, balance, composition. But still, in this course we do not teach children to be artists and do not study art history, painting, etc.

What kind of computer do you need? - Figma is accessible directly from the browser, so a basic computer or laptop that can run one of the modern browsers (Opera, Chrome, Yandex) is enough. The main thing is to have a stable Internet connection and a computer mouse. The touchpad will be uncomfortable

Do you need a graphics tablet? - Not. To work with Figma, a simple computer mouse is enough.
