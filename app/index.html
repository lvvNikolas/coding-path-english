How are the classes

1

At the agreed time, the student connects via video link to the teacher. If necessary, the issues that arose during the implementation of the home work are sorted out.

2

During the lesson, the child, under supervision, creates a project or part of a project, concurrently consolidating the learned knowledge or learning new ones.

3

At the end of the lesson, the results are summed up, questions are sorted out and homework is given, which teaches the student to independently solve problems.
