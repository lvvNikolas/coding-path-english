Roblox Game Making Course

A course to introduce children to programming and game development (game development). Allows you to learn the basics programming, study the creation of maps and landscapes, work with the logic of the game and just have fun and have fun. Roblox provides a wide range of tools for creating games. Scripts and logic inside roblox are written in lua .

What do we teach?

Creating buildings and structures, creating landscapes, placing lights on the map, setting up a global lighting. Writing various scripts: scripts for keys, elevators, change of day and night, collection items and creating trap logic .

Your questions

What are the benefits of roblox? - it gives a base and a strong foundation. Allows the child to be at ease and with interest in game development.

My child is playing roblox. Do you play in class? - Not. Roblox has a game client in which, most likely, your son or daughter plays. And there is a studio. roblox studio is a special development package, in which we create our projects. Therefore, in the classroom we we play, but from time to time we can run games to test it for performance.

What kind of computer do you need? - To work in roblox, an average laptop is enough. This program is not very demanding.
